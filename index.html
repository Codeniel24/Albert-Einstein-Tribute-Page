  • 1879 Born in Ulm, Kingdom of Wurttemberg, German Empire
  • 1900 Leaves his family to study in Federal Polytechnic school in Zurich
  • 1905 Has is PhD from the University of Zurich
  • 1903 Got married to Mileva Maric
  • 1919 He Divorced Mileva Maric
  • 1919 He Married again to Elsa Lowenthal
  • 1920 He was awarded Barnard Medal
  • 1921 He was awarded Nobel Prize in Physics
  • 1921 He was awarded Matteucci Medal and ForMemRS
  • 1925 He was awarded Copley Medal
  • 1926 He was awarded Gold Medal of the Royal Astronomical Society
  • 1929 He was awarded Max Planck Medal
  • 1955 He died in Princeton, New Jersey, US
